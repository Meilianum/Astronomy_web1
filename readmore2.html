Meteor Shower Peaks This Week

Don't miss this celestial event that will light up the night sky. Here's everything you need to know!

When and Where to Watch

The meteor shower will reach its peak on the weekend, and it will be visible across the globe. To get the best view, head to a dark location away from city lights. Use a blanket to lie on and enjoy the view for the best stargazing experience.

How to Spot a Meteor

Look towards the night sky in the direction of the constellation Perseus, as that is where most meteors will radiate from. You may see shooting stars every few minutes, so keep your eyes peeled!

Fun Facts About Meteor Showers

Meteor showers happen when Earth passes through the debris left behind by a comet. The dust and rock particles burn up in the atmosphere, creating those streaks of light we know as shooting stars. Enjoy this natural fireworks show!
